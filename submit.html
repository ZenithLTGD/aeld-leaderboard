Rule Guidelines

  1. You MUST add the ID of the level in the level name. Example: Future Funk (54356544)
  2. Please make the video proof ON YOUTUBE. Add a nice thumbnail of the level. You can make it public or unlisted on your channel.
  3. Please make the videos high-quality with clicks or a handcam.
  4. Completion should be accurate. Only enter numbers, no symbols. Example: 54. Bad example: 54% (NO PERCENT SYMBOL.)
  5. Show the full raw footage of the run.
  6. All submissions are reviewed. Invalid ones will be declined.
  7. Please follow these guidelines. If you don't, your record will instantly get declined.
